Product Overview

Pferd Polymers is a manufacturer and supplier of PTFE lined bends and elbows in various angles, including 90° and 45°. Our products are designed, processed and manufactured with high-quality paste-extruded PTFE liners in accordance with international standard ASTM F1545.

These lined fittings combine robust carbon steel or stainless steel housings with PTFE or antistatic PTFE liners for dependable corrosion resistance, high purity and long service life in aggressive process applications.

Quality Assurance

Testing & Inspection

Testing Specification Requirement
Visual / Dimensional Check 100% visual check / dimensional check.
Hydro Test 100% hydro test at 29.9 kg/cm2(g) for holding time of 3 mins. as per ASTM F 1545 - 15A.
Spark Test 100% spark test at 10 kV after lining.
Lining Thickness 100% lining thk. to be checked. (>80% of flaring thk.)

Important

Notes

  1. All elbow / bend fittings have SOFF flanges on both sides, with one loose flange and one fixed flange. Both-side loose flanges are available on request. For A105 flanges, a lap joint flange is provided at one end and a fixed flange at the other end.
  2. Suitable vent holes are provided for all fittings.
  3. All fittings work will be supplied with suitable wooden end covers to protect the flare faces.
  4. Material test certificates will be provided for all raw materials.
  5. The plastic liner flare will replace the raised face portion of the flange.
