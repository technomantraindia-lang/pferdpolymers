Product Overview

Pferd Polymers is a manufacturer and supplier of FEP / PFA lined equal and unequal tees in various standard and non-standard sizes. We also manufacture PFA / FEP / HDPE lined tees in accordance with international standard ASTM F1545.

Our lined tees combine seamless steel housings with high-performance polymer liners for corrosion resistance, high purity and dependable service in chemical, pharmaceutical and allied process industries.

Industries Served

Quality Assurance

Testing & Inspection

Testing Specification Requirement
Visual / Dimensional Check 100% visual check / dimensional check.
Hydro Test 100% hydro test at 29.9 kg/cm2(g) for holding time of 3 mins. as per ASTM F 1545 - 15A.
Spark Test 100% spark test at 10 kV after lining.
Lining Thickness 100% lining thk. to be checked. (>80% of flaring thk.)

Important

Notes

  1. All tees have SOFF flanges on both sides, with one loose flange and one fixed flange. Both-side loose flanges are available on request. For A105 flanges, a lap joint flange is provided at one end and a fixed flange at the other end.
  2. Suitable vent holes are provided for all fittings.
  3. All fittings work will be supplied with suitable wooden end covers to protect the flare faces.
  4. Material test certificates will be provided for all raw materials.
  5. The plastic liner flare will replace the raised face portion of the flange.
