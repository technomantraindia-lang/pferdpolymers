Lined Elbow / Bend

Pferd Polymers manufactures and supplies PTFE lined elbows and bends in 90°, 45° and custom angles — built for corrosive chemical and pharmaceutical process lines with ASTM F1545 compliance.

Available Sizes:
20 NB to 300 NB (3/4" to 12")

90°, 45° (60°, 30° on request)
Lining Materials:
PTFE / PFA / FEP / HDPE
Lining Standard:

Pressure Rating:
ASA 150# / 300# / DIN / Table F
Temperature Range:
-29°C to +200°C

Product Overview

Pferd Polymers is a manufacturer and supplier of PTFE lined bends and elbows in various angles — 90°, 45° and 60°, 30° on request. We also manufacture PFA / FEP / HDPE lined elbows and bends using advanced and innovative lining techniques for chemical, pharmaceutical and allied industries.

For over a decade we have been designing, processing and manufacturing the highest quality paste-extruded PTFE liners for our products. All lined elbows and bends conform to international standard ASTM F1545. We pride ourselves on high levels of customer service and high-integrity products.

Important

Notes

  1. All elbow / bend have SOFF flange both sides with one end loose flange and fixed flange at the other end. (Both side loose flange on request.) For A105 flange: lap joint flange at one end and fixed flange at the other end.
  2. Suitable vent holes are provided for all fittings.
  3. All fittings work will be supplied with suitable wooden end covers to protect the flare faces.
  4. Material test certificates will be provided for all raw materials.
  5. The plastic liner flare will replace the raised face portion of the flange.
