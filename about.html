Who We Are

With extensive expertise in fluoropolymer lining technologies and process equipment manufacturing, PFERD Polymers serves a diverse range of industries including chemical processing, oil & gas, petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, water treatment, fertilizers, power generation, and specialty manufacturing. Our PTFE, PFA, and FEP lined products are engineered to provide exceptional chemical resistance, product purity, operational safety, and long service life under the most challenging operating conditions.

At PFERD Polymers, we combine advanced manufacturing technologies with stringent quality control systems to produce world-class lined piping systems, fittings, valves, vessels, columns, and custom-engineered equipment. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every stage of our operations, from material selection and engineering design to manufacturing, testing, and after-sales support.

Our team of experienced engineers and technical specialists works closely with customers to understand their unique process requirements and deliver customized solutions tailored to their applications.
