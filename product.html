Product Overview

PTFE / PFA / FEP lined pipes consist of a metallic outer shell lined with high-performance fluoropolymers to provide exceptional resistance against corrosive chemicals, high purity, and extreme temperatures. These pipes combine the strength of steel with the inertness of fluoropolymers, ensuring long service life and reduced maintenance.

Industries Served:

  • Chemical Processing
  • Petrochemical Plants
  • Pharmaceutical Industry
  • Food & Beverage
  • Water Treatment
