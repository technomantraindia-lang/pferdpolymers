Product Overview

Pferd Polymers specializes in the supply of PTFE-lined piping systems for the chemical, pharmaceutical and related industries. PTFE fluoropolymers offer exceptional chemical inertness and virtually universal corrosion resistance, making them suitable for transporting highly corrosive and hazardous process media. These systems are also widely used in high-purity applications and as a cost-effective alternative to alloy materials such as Hastelloy.

As a growing manufacturer and stockist of PTFE-lined piping products, Pferd Polymers provides a comprehensive range of solutions for demanding industrial environments. We also manufacture and supply PFA and FEP lined fittings using advanced production methods. Our expertise covers the design, engineering and production of premium paste-extruded PTFE, FEP and PFA lined products in accordance with ASTM F1545. We remain committed to dependable products, customer support and integrity in every aspect of our business.

Quality Assurance

Testing & Inspection

Testing Specification Requirement
Visual / Dimensional Check 100% visual check / dimensional check.
Hydro Test 100% hydro test at 29.9 kg/cm2(g) for holding time of 3 mins. as per ASTM F 1545 - 15A.
Spark Test 100% spark test at 10 kV after lining.
Lining Thickness 100% lining thk. to be checked. (>80% of flaring thk.)

Important

Notes

  1. All pipes have SOFF flange both sides with one end loose flange and fixed flange at the other end. For A105 flange: lap joint flange at one end and fixed flange at the other end.
  2. Lined pipes are available up to a length of 6 meters max.
  3. All fittings are supplied with fixed cast or welded flanges only.
  4. Suitable vent holes are provided for all pipes & fittings.
  5. All pipe work will be supplied with suitable wooden end covers to protect the flare faces.
  6. Material test certificates will be provided for all raw materials.
  7. Weld buttons will be provided for pipe spools more than 300 mm long.
  8. The plastic liner flare will replace the raised face portion of the flange.
